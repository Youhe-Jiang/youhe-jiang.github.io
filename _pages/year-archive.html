Beyond research

What keeps me balanced outside systems work.

Research takes a lot of concentration, but I do not want life to collapse into papers, experiments, and cluster logs. Movement, rhythm, and physical training matter to me because they keep both attention and energy in good shape.

Dance

Dance is one of the main ways I reset and connect.

I enjoy dancing not only as personal expression, but also as a way to learn with other people. The social side of movement, timing, and exchange is part of what makes it meaningful to me.

Training

Exercise helps me maintain consistency.

I care about staying physically active. Regular exercise gives structure to the week, improves focus, and counterbalances the long stretches of deep technical work that research usually demands.

How it connects

Outside interests are not separate from research for me. They influence how I think about discipline, iteration, and improving under constraints, which is also how I approach systems work.
